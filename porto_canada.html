Forum

PortoCanada was made with the main focus to unite and help portuguese speaker people that live in Canada.

Immigration can be hard sometimes to adapt to, that is why a place where people can get some guidance and help each other is so important, it may save a lot of time and change people life by letting them know what they weren't aware of.

The website is already deployed and working at portocanada.com
